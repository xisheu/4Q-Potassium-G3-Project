Game Details

Mechanics:
- There are three types of difficulty levels and five branches of math. The player will get to choose what difficulty level and branch of Math to have a quiz on.

RULES:
- ﻿﻿Once you get an item wrong, one life is deducted.
- ﻿﻿Once you skip a question, you will lose a life
- You can any choose one option.

DIFFICULTY LEVELS:
EASY:
- ﻿﻿FIVE (5) LIVES; YOU GAIN 1 POINT FOR Each CORRECT ANSWER.
- ﻿﻿NO TIMER; 1O QUESTIONS
MEDIUM:
- ﻿FOUR (4) LIVES; YOU GAIN 2 POINTS FOR EACH CORRECT ANSWER.
- ﻿﻿TIMER OF 10 MINUTES; 8 QUESTIONS
HARD:
- ﻿﻿THREE (3) LIVES; YOU GAIN 3 POINTS FOR EACH CORRECT ANSWER
- ﻿TIMER OF 5 MINUTES: 3 QUESTIONS

BRANCHES OF MATH:
Algebra
Geometry
Statistics
Trigonometry
Number Theory

PLAYER COUNT: SINGLE PLAYER
